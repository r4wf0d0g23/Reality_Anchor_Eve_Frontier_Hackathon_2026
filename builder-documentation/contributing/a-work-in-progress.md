# A Work In Progress

As we transition EVE Frontier to the Sui Blockchain we are also re-writing all of our documentation to reflect this change and expanding the scope of this site to better serve those new to building in EVE Frontier.&#x20;

This will be a gradual process and you will notice //todo's on a number of pages, these pages are still being actively worked on and will be filled out over the coming days and weeks.&#x20;

That's not the end however; and this new documentation site has been designed for community contribution going forward, so that together, we can build a better experience for builders at all skill levels.&#x20;

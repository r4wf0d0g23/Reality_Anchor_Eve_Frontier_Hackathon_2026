## Help Test the EVE Vault App on iOS and Android

Help us test the EVE Vault app for both Android and iOS devices.

### iOS (via TestFlight)

To test the EVE Vault app on iOS, please follow these steps:

1. **Download TestFlight**  
   First, install the TestFlight app from the App Store on your iOS device. You can find it [here](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664).

2. **Join the Beta Test**  
   After installing TestFlight, open this link on your mobile device to access the EVE Vault test:  
   [Join EVE Vault Beta v1.0.5](https://testflight.apple.com/join/w2NCeawN)

3. **Start Testing**  
   Follow the prompts in TestFlight to download and install the app. You’ll be notified of future updates through TestFlight, allowing you to easily keep the app up to date with new test releases.

### Android (via APK Download)

To test the EVE Vault app on Android, follow these steps:

1. **Download the APK**  
   Visit this link on your Android device to download the EVE Vault APK:  
   [Download EVE Vault APK v1.0.5](https://artifacts.evefrontier.com/wallet/android/eve-vault-v1.0.5.apk)

2. **Install the App**  
   You may need to manually allow the installation of apps from Chrome or another browser by adjusting your device’s security settings. Once downloaded, open the APK file to install the app.

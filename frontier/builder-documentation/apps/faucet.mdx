# GAS Faucet
In this guide, you will be able to get GAS in **Eve Frontier.**

## Pre-Requisites
To follow this guide, you need to make sure to follow both the [Wallet Game Setup](/EveVault/walletSetup) and the [Browser Extension](/EveVault/installation) guides.

## Guide

<Steps>
### Step 1: Visit
Head to the OP Sepolia faucet hosted by GHOST: [ghostchain.io/faucet/ethereum-sepolia](https://ghostchain.io/faucet/ethereum-sepolia/) and click on <strong>"Connect Wallet"</strong>.
<br/ ><br/ >![](./assets/faucet/faucet1.png)

### Step 2: Connect Wallet
Next, select One Key as your Wallet. This is the EVE Vault wallet.
<br/ ><br/ >![](./assets/faucet/faucet2.png)

### Step 3: Authorize
There should be a new popup window that asks to connect to the website. Press the Connect button.
<br/ ><br/ >![](./assets/faucet/faucet3.png)

### Step 4: Receive
Now your wallet should be connect, you can select the Receive button.
<br/ ><br/ >![](./assets/faucet/faucet4.png)

### Step 5: Confirm
Now, you can go to the **EVE Vault** either in the game, or in your browser extension. You should see roughly 0.01 additional Sepolia ETH (the OP Sepolia testnet gas token).
<br/ ><br/ >![](./assets/faucet/faucet5.png)

</Steps>
